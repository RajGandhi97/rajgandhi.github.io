Modularity of Calabi-Yau Varieties

Together with Colin Ingalls and Adam Logan, I have been searching for weight 4 newforms not yet known to appear in the L-series of rigid Calabi-Yau threefolds. It is known that the L-series of any rigid Calabi-Yau threefold equals the L-series of some weight 4 newform, but the correspondence is only conjectured to be bijective. We use a strategy of Burek's paper and count points over finite fields on quotients of modular Calabi-Yau threefolds discovered in a dissertation of Meyer. We compare these point counts to coefficients of weight 4 modular forms, with hopes to find rigid correspondences. The ZIP file contains magma script and output of results so far: ZIP.
